# DARWIN Stage 6 doctrine

Date: `2026-04-09`

Status:

- Stage 5 is closed and remains the baseline.
- Stage 6 is a new program.

## Thesis

Stage 6 exists to prove that promoted families can be activated across a broader bounded transfer matrix and measurably improve later search economics without losing interpretability.

## What Stage 6 is

- a broader-transfer and family-activation program
- an economics-truth tightening program for live claim-bearing rows
- a bounded compounding program on the current lane set

## What Stage 6 is not

- not “more Stage 5”
- not lane expansion first
- not composition-first
- not a stealth async/distributed agenda
- not a runtime-truth rewrite

## Lane posture

- `lane.systems` remains the primary proving lane
- `lane.repo_swe` remains the challenge lane
- `lane.scheduling` is the bounded transfer confirmation target
- `lane.harness` remains watchdog/control only
- `lane.atp` remains audit-only
- `lane.research` stays closed unless a later narrow consumer slice is explicitly authorized

## Primitive posture

- keep `ExecutionEnvelopeV3`; tighten semantics before renaming
- keep `SearchPolicyV2`; refine operational inputs before considering `V3`
- keep `ComponentFamilyV2`; make it operational rather than ceremonial
- extend `CompoundingCaseV1` with stronger controls
- add `FamilyActivationV1`
- add `ComparisonEnvelopeV1` as a subobject
- add `TransferCaseV2` as a typed transfer record

## Core controls

- `cold_start`
- `warm_start`
- `family_lockout`
- `single_family_lockout`
- `delayed_activation`
- bounded `activation_probe`

## Hard anti-goals

- do not broaden campaign count or family count ahead of activation lift
- do not authorize composition before broader transfer is real
- do not add a new domain lane early
- do not widen execution-envelope consumption casually
- do not let builder scripts become the source of family or transfer truth

## Stage-6 success condition

Stage 6 succeeds only if family activation and broader transfer measurably improve later search outcomes under matched live economics and clean comparison truth.
