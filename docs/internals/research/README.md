# Research Internals

This section is for maintainer-facing research closeout material that still
belongs in tracked docs:

- stop/go syntheses
- freeze and deferral notes
- phase-0 pressure packets
- tranche closeout gates that explain why a subsystem stopped growing

These notes are useful when you are maintaining or extending BreadBoard's
research systems. They are not the right starting point for ordinary adoption
or day-to-day usage.

Public subsystem overviews and operator playbooks stay under `docs/concepts/`
and `docs/guides/`. Local planning artifacts and execution archaeology still
belong in `docs_tmp/`.
