# DARWIN Phase-1 Signoff

Date: 2026-03-14

## Scope

Phase-1 DARWIN closes with:

- six live lanes on shared Darwin contracts
- four search-enabled lanes
- promotion-capable lineage control with replay-audited promotion history
- first valid cross-lane transfer protocol
- compute-normalized comparative companion
- comparative dossier and final internal evidence bundle

## What is validated

- canonical orchestration run emits the full artifact chain
- readiness is `overall_ok=true`
- baseline scorecard is `overall_ok=true`
- replay audit completed for:
  - promoted scheduling candidate
  - promoted transfer-derived research candidate
  - representative repo_swe search candidate

## What is claimable

- Darwin Phase-1 is operational as an internal multi-lane evolutionary platform
- six lanes emit shared control/evaluation artifacts
- four lanes support bounded typed-search execution
- promotion control and transfer protocol are operational under bounded internal rules

## What is not claimable

- external superiority claims
- publication-grade transfer claims
- broad compute-normalized superiority claims
- frontier-grade comparative claims

## Phase boundary

Anything beyond this point is Phase-2 work:

- stronger compute-normalized comparative interpretation
- broader transfer families
- richer multi-tranche promotion policy
- final publication-grade or external-safe evidence packets
