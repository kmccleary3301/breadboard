## Objective

Turn Stage-4 deep-live-search evidence into the first canonical family program:

- first promoted family on `lane.repo_swe`
- first promoted or explicitly withheld family on `lane.systems`
- first bounded retained transfer
- first informative failed-transfer taxonomy
- first canonical Stage-4 family registry

## Lane roles

- source/proving: `lane.repo_swe`, `lane.systems`
- watchdog only: `lane.harness`
- bounded confirmation/failure lane: `lane.scheduling`
- audit only: `lane.atp`
- deferred: `lane.research`

## Hard boundaries

This tranche does not authorize:

- async or distributed work
- broad lane expansion
- broad transfer sweeps
- public or superiority claims
- archive-as-truth behavior
- another deep-search expansion by inertia
